Structured Light

This group of work explores and develops a relationship between code and material. The forms act as a kind of physical code to create a flat image, playing both metaphorical and functional roles. The series demonstrates this connection through parametric modeling and fabrication processes utilizing laser cutting, 3D printing, and CNC milling.
