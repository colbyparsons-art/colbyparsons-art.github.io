Semiotic

This group of work features small vignettes or still lifes based on actual objects or groups of things encountered in daily life. Projected video collages combine found imagery depicting the rendered objects or thematically connected content — source material primarily internet-collected, representing a collective unconscious filtered through personal selection and associative thinking.
