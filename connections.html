Connections

This group of work is a further exploration of algorithmically generated forms, consisting of simple sheets of material, connected by randomly positioned linkages, using simple materials such as plywood, cardboard, plexiglas and copper.

Porcelain, acrylic, chipboard, copper sheet

Acrylic, MDF, neoprene
