Materiality of Light

This group of work represents an exploration of the distortion and textural qualities of video projection in intersection with physical form — particularly clay forms with a strong sense of materiality. The pixel pattern inherent to the projection process is treated as analogous to a kind of materiality: a fundamental quality of the medium that can be celebrated and exploited as an aesthetic of authenticity.
