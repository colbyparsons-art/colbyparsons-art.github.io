About

Colby Parsons applies aspects of traditional media approaches to digital-based processes, working in a range of media and formats including ceramics, digital fabrication, projection mapping, installation, and interactivity.

He is a Full Professor in the Visual Arts Division at Texas Woman's University, where he has taught Ceramics and Digital Craft since 1998. His work has been exhibited nationally and internationally, and he has completed artist residencies in Taiwan, Philadelphia, and Denmark.
